Facebook Messenger Chatbot

Due to the lack of adoption previous GraphWhy applications have faced the chatbot was an attempt to make application engagement convient for our users. The idea behind developing a chatbot was conduct weekly surveys and use the messenger to ensure, votes and route results back to users. The Chatbot is passively animated by API.ai and hosted through Node.js. API.ai functions as an About Us, and the UX infostructure. Node.js connects API.ai with Messenger and houses a backend CMS so that we can send specific messages and images to users.
